Quick Guide

  1. Set your location above the map (type it, or tap "Pick on map").
  2. Open Hunt Setup — check store types, tap HUNT!.
  3. Tap a map pin, or an item in Route Details, to see that store's card.
  4. Open Route Details to set Start/End, generate your route, and send it to Google Maps.
  5. Track wishlist cars in 🔥 Hot List.
  6. On your phone in Chrome, open the browser menu and tap "Install and create shortcut" to add this to your home screen.
